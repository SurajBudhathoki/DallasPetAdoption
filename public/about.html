About us


Dallas Pet Adoption is a non-profit organization based in Dallas- Fort Worth, Texas which was launched in 2018. Our organization main goal is to make sure the animals find suitable homes.

Mission

Our mission is to increase public awareness about the availability of high-quality adoptable pets and increase the overall effectiveness of pet adoption programs.

Our website allows pet lovers search pets that best match their needs. There is various information available and users are encouraged to discover what services we offer.

Core Values


  • Respect
  • Commitment
  • Positivity
  • Honesty
  • Excellent Service

FAQ

How do I search for a pet?

  • You can begin your search immediately by using the browse tab above which will route you to a list of all pets in our database. You will be able to choose the pets based on type or breed.

    • How often is the website updated?

  • The website is updated at the end of the day everyday as we continue to add a list of pets daily.

    • Do i need to apply to Dallas Pet Adoption?

  • No. Once you have made a request for more information, our reps will reach out with you to set up a quick meetup to determite whether it's a match.

    • How long does it take to hear back?

  • Once a request is made, our reps reach out to you within 3-5 business days.

    • Where can i get more detailed information about a pet?

  • In order to receive more detailed information, click the contact tab and once we receive your message we can send you the detailed information.
